What I do

Digital Strategy & Leadership

With a proven track record leading cross-functional teams, I drive audience growth and engagement through data-informed content strategies. I’ve overseen SEO/SEM campaigns that boosted organic traffic by 20%, owned editorial strategy for high-performing newsletters with open rates over 65%, and led digital rebranding initiatives that improved UX and recirculation. Whether managing websites or mentoring producers, I bring a strategic lens to every digital decision.

Content Development & UX Research

My work blends storytelling, design, and UX. I craft engaging multimedia content using Adobe Creative Suite, Final Cut Pro, Figma, and Avid. I’ve researched user experience and developed audience-focused editorial strategies, particularly for Northeastern Global News. From developing interactive stories to designing marketing brochures, I ensure content resonates with users and business objectives.

Analytics & Performance Optimization

Skilled in analytics and reporting tools like Google Analytics, SEMrush, Tableau, Hotjar, and Chartbeat, I specialize in measuring and optimizing performance. I’ve used data to refine content strategies, increase engagement metrics, and inform A/B testing across digital channels. I also have technical fluency in Python, SQL, R, and JavaScript, allowing me to dive deeper into data and build impactful solutions that align with KPIs.
